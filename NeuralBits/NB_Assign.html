About NeuralBits

Founded in 2013 in Mumbai, NeuralBits Technologies Pvt Ltd has been a leader in homegrown IT solutions for Indian healthcare systems. Our mission is to build powerful, affordable healthcare platforms that enable digital transformation for clinics, hospitals, and public health institutions.

With cloud-based HIS systems, advanced OPD/IPD modules, and custom software for government projects, we combine deep technical know-how with healthcare insight.

For over a decade, NeuralBits has been providing state-of-the-art software for hospitals, Nursing Homes and Clinics. Most healthcare software systems rely on outdated client server technologies accessible only from network connected desktop computers installed on premises. NeuralBits fundamentally changes how you manage your Clinic / Hospitals. With NeuralBits, you get a fully integrated healthcare management suite delivered on a cloud-based platform providing you a robust, secure and available solution for your needs.
